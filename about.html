ABOUT ME

As a third-year student of Industrial Engineering and Management, my journey is further enriched by a robust athletic background, which has cultivated a myriad of skills essential for both academic and professional success. This unique blend of experiences has imbued me with an unwavering drive, effective management capabilities, and a collaborative spirit that thrives in team settings.

WORK EXPERIENCE

As a dedicated athlete, I have had the privilege of playing professional basketball since 2016, currently representing Hapoel Ramat Gan Givatayim in the third division. From 2014 to 2018, I had the honor of representing my country as a member of the Israeli Basketball National Youth Team. From 2018 to 2021, I managed and coached basketball teams at the Maccabi Hadera Basketball Club.

EDUCATION

Since 2021, I've been studying Industrial Engineering and Management at Ruppin Academic Center, expanding my knowledge and skills. My curiosity about finance led me to complete a Capital Market and Stock Exchange Trading Course at Psagot College in 2020. I'm not just passionate about academics; basketball has been a significant part of my life. In 2018, I took a Basketball Coaching Course at Lewinsky-Wingate College to understand the game from a coaching perspective. My journey started at the Wingate Institute (2013-2016), where I balanced high school with intensive basketball training at their Basketball Academy. These experiences have taught me discipline, teamwork, and the importance of pursuing excellence.

MILITARY SERVICE

From 2016 to 2018, I served in the military, where I was recognized as an outstanding athlete. During this time, I played for the Israeli national basketball team, participating in European championships and international tournaments, and even played professionally in the second league. Alongside my sports achievements, I played a crucial role in the Logistics Unit. There, I was in charge of managing supply warehouses, juggling various tasks, and collaborating closely with military officials. This period was not only about showcasing my skills on the basketball court but also about contributing significantly to the logistical operations of the military.
